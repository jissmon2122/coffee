// Alternative SMS providers - Free options

## Free SMS Platforms

### 1. AWS SNS (Amazon Simple Notification Service)
- **Free Tier:** 100 SMS per month FREE
- **After:** $0.00645 per SMS (US)
- **Setup:** 5 minutes
- **Best for:** Businesses already using AWS

### 2. Vonage (formerly Nexmo)
- **Free Credits:** $2 USD to start
- **Cost:** $0.07-0.10 per SMS
- **Setup:** 5 minutes
- **Best for:** Small volume

### 3. Termii (African SMS)
- **Free:** 50 SMS per month
- **Cost:** Pay-as-you-go after
- **Setup:** 5 minutes
- **Best for:** Africa region

### 4. MSG91
- **Free:** 50 SMS for testing
- **Cost:** Variable by region
- **Setup:** 5 minutes
- **Best for:** India & Asia

### 5. Bandwidth
- **Free Trial:** $10 credit
- **Cost:** $0.0075 per SMS
- **Setup:** 10 minutes
- **Best for:** US businesses

### 6. Africa's Talking (Africast)
- **Free:** 50 SMS per month
- **Cost:** Pay-as-you-go
- **Setup:** 5 minutes
- **Best for:** Africa

### 7. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)
- **Free:** Included with email
- **Cost:** Variable
- **Setup:** 5 minutes
- **Best for:** Email + SMS combo

---

## Recommendation: AWS SNS (BEST FREE OPTION)

**Why AWS SNS:**
- ✅ 100 FREE SMS per month (first 12 months)
- ✅ Most reliable
- ✅ Used by major companies
- ✅ Easy to setup
- ✅ Can scale up later
